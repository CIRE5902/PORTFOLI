Hello, I'm Eric – Web Developer

My name is Eric —although my friends call me “Cirelema”—, I'm 22 years old and from Barcelona, Spain. At first, I considered pursuing a career in the animal care sector, as it's always been one of my greatest passions. However, after an internship, I discovered that it also has very tough moments, so I decided to make the leap into programming.

Since then, I've enjoyed creating intuitive and visually appealing web experiences. I'm passionate about the visual side and interface design, because I believe aesthetics are key to delivering the best possible user experience.

I completed my studies as a Web Developer and Systems Administrator. During my Higher Vocational Training, I stood out by helping my classmates learn CSS — sharing knowledge and promoting good design are essential values for me.

I now have experience developing web applications, and my goal is to combine my two greatest passions —programming and the animal world— by creating projects that help improve the lives of animals.

Outside of tech, I’m passionate about the world of motor vehicles, sports, fashion in my own style, and animals. I also value solitude, but I truly enjoy spending time with my family and close friends. Emotional and inspiring movies, especially those with a meaningful message, are my favorites — like The Iron Giant, The Lord of the Rings, or I Am Legend.
